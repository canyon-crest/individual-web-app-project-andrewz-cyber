Hades is a roguelike action game developed by Supergiant Games. The game was released in September 2020 for Microsoft Windows, macOS, and Linux. It's availble on multiple platforms, such as Nintendo Switch, PS5, XBox, PC, MacOS, etc. It received widespread critical acclaim for its gameplay, story, and overall design. I really loved the game when I played it, and I highly recommend it to anyone. This website is a short showcase of the different characters and weapons that the player encounters throughout their journey through the underworld.
